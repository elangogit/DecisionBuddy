Dezide

We help you take decisions

View project onGitHub

Don't put a lot of things in your mind, we will do it for you by persisting your mind on your phone

Have you ever felt the need to track your thoughts before you made a decision. Now you have a tool which can help you do that.

"Dezide" is the first and only tool in App Store that helps you create, track and share decision.

Features

  1. Create decisions that you would like to track over a period of time, the best suited decisions for using Dezide sounds like "Should I ....."
  2. Use the application every day to record your decision based on what you feel at that time about the decision.
  3. At the end of time period, Dezide will summarize your decision. It will substitute the default option (specified during decision creation) for days you had skipped taking a call.
  4. Decision results can be shared with friends using Facebook.
